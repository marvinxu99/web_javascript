SINGLE-ENGINE FAILURE/FLAMEOUT

Symptoms:

  1. TGT, Ng, torque, and Np on malfunctioning engine decreasing toward zero.
  2. NO. 1 ENG OUT or NO. 2 ENG OUT warning light illuminated.

Corrective Action:

  1. Nr - MAINTAIN.
  2. CONTGCY PWR - ON.
  3. Altitude/Airspeed - AS REQUIRED.
  4. Fuel/stores - JETTISON AS REQUIRED.
  5. ENG Anti-ice - AS REQUIRED.
  6. Analyze.

If no indication of mechanical malfunction or engine fire:

  1. Perform Engine Restart Procedure.

If Engine Restart Procedure is not to be performed:

  1. Conduct EMER ENG SHUTDOWN.
  2. LAND AS SOON AS PRACTICABLE.
